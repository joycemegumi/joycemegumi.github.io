Fashion Trend Clustering Tool

When I was deciding on my first data science project I wanted it to be related to the fashion industry because of my background in fashion at FIT.

One day I came across a simple problem that my classmate had brought up when talking about her internship experience at Coach, as an Assistant Womens Retail Merchandiser. She would spend days collecting images of similar trends across various major fashion brands by screenshotting them to compile them into a deck for the merchandising catalog plan for the next season.

I was so shocked to hear that a major brand like Coach would still have such a manual process for trend forecasting.

So I thought maybe trend forecasting services like WGSN has a tool like this?

Nope.

Well kinda. But very limited.

They claim to be personalized with filters but they don't let you choose which sites you want your images to come from. They just tell you the trend type and what brands are doing it. But what if you have a particular brand image and you don't want to be following trends that Free People (Boho chic style) has but you want to look at trends from brands that like Zara (Trendy modern woman style)?

Not to mention WGSN's services cost a whopping Â£16,500 in annual subscription fees. Yikes!

So I decided to make a trend clustering tool that solves this problem!
